Important information

To solve the homework problems, there are several areas that need to be understood. To help, the following sections are available:

  • Objective: summarizes what the question is asking.
  • Key Equations: lists the equations that may help in solving and understanding the problem.
  • References: lists the pages of the book where explanations of the concepts in the homework can be found. It may also include outside references that may be useful.
  • Things to Think About: covers tricky aspects of the question that may lead to incorrect answers. Thinking about these may help in solving the question faster.
