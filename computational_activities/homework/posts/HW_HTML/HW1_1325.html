    3. There are a few ways to call a SciPy constant:
      1. constants.[constant]. Example: q1 = 2*constants.e assigns 2 times the elementary charge to the q1 variable. This way pertains to the Physical and Mathematical constants listed at the top of the SciPy constants page (SciPy Constants).
      2. constants.value(u'[constant]'). Example: p = constants.value(u'proton mass') assigns the proton mass constant to the variable p. These constants are listed under the Constants Database on the linked page.
