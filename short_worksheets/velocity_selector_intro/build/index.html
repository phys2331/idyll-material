  2. Imagine that you want electrons moving 3×1063\times10^63×106 m/s to feel no force. If the magnetic field magnitude is 1 T and the electric field magnitude is 10 kN/C, will the force on these electrons cancel?

    No. The magnetic force is greater than the electric force, so the net force will be in the positive x-direction.
